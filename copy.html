This restaurant was inspired by Ichiraku Ramen, the iconic anime restaurant from the Naruto series. 🍜

Ichiraku Ramen is a cozy ramen joint located in the hidden village of Konohagakure. Founded by Teuchi, it quickly became a favorite among the townspeople. But what makes it truly special is that it’s also Naruto Uzumaki’s go-to spot for a hearty meal. Between his ninja missions, Naruto often stops by to indulge in their delicious ramen bowls.

The shop’s popularity extends beyond the anime world. In fact, the real-world inspiration for Ichiraku Ramen was a noodle shop near Fukuoka’s Kyuusandai-mae Station. Although the original Ichiraku closed in 2014, other branches still exist in Fukuoka. One such branch, the Shime location near Fukuoka Airport, serves mouthwatering ramen dishes. The Miso Chashu Pork Ramen (¥850 or approximately US $8.48) is a must-try. Imagine a rich miso broth with tender chashu pork, bean sprouts, and green onions—all served in a unique bowl resembling a mortar and pestle. 🌟

Whether you’re a Naruto fan or simply a ramen enthusiast, Ichiraku Ramen offers a delightful culinary experience. So, if you find yourself in Fukuoka, don’t miss the chance to savor a taste of this beloved anime restaurant! 😊👍
